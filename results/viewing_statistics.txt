Hypothesis Testing: Temporal Analysis
----------------------------------------
Null Hypothesis: The distributions of video watching duration are the same across all months.
Alternative Hypothesis: The distributions of video watching duration differ across months.

Note: Using Kruskal-Wallis H test instead of ANOVA because:
1. The data is not normally distributed
2. There are significant outliers in the viewing durations
3. The test makes no assumptions about the distribution of the data

Kruskal-Wallis H Test Results:
H-statistic: 2253.8504
P-value: p < 0.001
Conclusion: Reject the null hypothesis: There are significant differences in viewing duration distributions across months.
